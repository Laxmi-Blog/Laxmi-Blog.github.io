My Hero Academia boycotted in China

2020-02-14 记事/Memo ， English/英文

From fandom.com

My Hero Academia (僕のヒーローアカデミア Boku no Hīrō Akademia?) is a Manga series serialized by Shonen Jump and written by Kohei Horikoshi. The series has been adapted into an Anime series and an Anime Movie, as well as spawning the Spinoff series My Hero Academia Vigilantes.

The story is set in the modern-day, except people with special powers called "Quirks" have become commonplace throughout the world. In a world of chaos and confusion, Pro Heroes have now risen as the new profession to fight off powerful super villains.

On "Weekly Boys JUMP" released on February 3, 2020, in the comic 259 words, the author named a villain doing human experiments as Shiga Maruta, and gave Maruta the pronunciation. "Shiga" was taken from Japanese bacteriologist Shiga Jie and is also used as the name for "Shiga bacillus". "Shigella" is also known as "dysentery bacillus" and has been used in human experiments on the "July 31" unit. Maruta in Japanese means roundwood, which means "test product", and it is an insulting name for the victims of the "July Trinity" bacterial unit of the Japanese invaders who were forced to undergo human experiments.
Only one day after the comic was released, the Bilibili score of the work dropped to 2.3 (minimum 2.0). Subsequently, the Japanese publisher Shueisha issued a statement calling this an "unintentional mistake" without apologizing, which made netizens even more angry. On February 7th, the original author Ying Yue Gengping and Jiyingsha jointly issued an apology and promised to change the name. In response, many readers criticized: "No sincerity!" And adopted different methods to resist.

Apology from author's twitter
Apology for expression in "Weekly Shonen Jump" 2020 No. 10 "My Hero Academia" Translated by Google

Appeared in "My Hero Academia" episode 259 published in "Weekly Shonen Jump" 2020 No. 10 (released on February 3) Many readers from China and abroad have pointed out that the character name "Shigamaruta" is reminiscent of memories of the tragic history of the past. "Shiga" was named after some of the other characters, and "Maruta" was named after its appearance, with no intent to overlap with past history. However, the combination of these characters and the setting of the character "Doctor of evil organization" has been annoying to readers overseas, especially in China. The editorial department should have done a thorough examination of the expression in advance. Deeply apologize. Taking this issue as a serious hit, the character name will be changed promptly in the electronic version when recording comics in the paper version, and in order to prevent such a situation from happening in the future, understanding various history and culture We will continue to strive to deepen our manga, and we will continue to deliver cartoons that can be loved by everyone while snuggling with the feelings of many people.

February 7, 2020 Shueisha Co., Ltd.

"My Hero Academia" The name "Shiga log" that I drew in episode 259 made many people feel very uncomfortable, sorry. "Shiga" became enthusiastic about the former "All For One" boss of the "Enemy Coalition (Villan Coalition)", and his real name "Death" A part of the pattern is called "Shiga," and "log" is intended to express the feeling of "chubby and fat." Both were accidental and had no intent to hurt the readers. I sincerely apologize. In the future, we will make every effort not to repeat such a thing.

Apology from author's twitter (original language)
「週刊少年ジャンプ」2020年10号「僕のヒーローアカデミア 』における表現についてのお詫び

「週刊少年ジャンプ』 2020年10号（2月3日発売）掲載の「僕のヒーローアカデミア 』第259話に登場したキャラクター名「志賀丸太（しがまるた）」について、過去の悲惨な歴史の記憶を想起させるとのご指摘を、中国をはじめとする海外の読者の方々から多くいただきました。「志質（しが）」は他の登場人物名の一部から、「丸太（まるた）」はその外見から命名したもので、過去の歴史と重ね合わせる意図は全くありませんでした。しかしながら、「悪の組織の医師」というキャラクターの設定とこれらの名前が合わさった結果、中国をはじめとする海外の読者の皆様に不快な思いをさせてしまいました。事前に編集部が表現について十全な検討を行うべきでした。深くお詫び申し上げます。今回の問題を真撃に受け止め、当該キャラクター名を、紙版においてはコミックス収録時に、電子版においてはすみやかに変更いたします今後、このような事態を起こさないよう、様々な歴史と文化への理解を深める努力を続けるとともに、多くの人々の気持ちに寄り添いながら、皆様に愛きれる漫画をお届けしてまいります。

2020年2月7日株式会社集英社

「僕のヒーローアカデミア」第259話で描いた「志賀丸太」という名前について、大勢の方に大変不快な思いをさせてしまい、誠に申し訳ございません。「志賀（しが）」は、彼がかつての「敵連合（ヴィラン連合）」のボス「オール・フォー・ワン」に心酔し、自らその存在に近づきたいと思ったことから、その本名「死柄木（レがらき）」の一部をとって「志質（しが）」と名乗ることにしたもので、「丸太」は「丸々と太った」感じを表せればと名付けたものです。いずれも偶然で、読者のみなさんを傷つける意図は全くありませんでした。心からお詫びいたします。今後、二度とこのようなことを繰り返さぬよう、努めてまいります。
